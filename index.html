Why I Do This

I started this because too many brands are buying blind.

I've worked inside multi-million dollar retail and manufacturing operations — the kind with 50+ locations, four warehouses, and thousands of SKUs. And I kept seeing the same thing: purchasing decisions based on gut feel, not data.

Overbuying that trapped cash. Stockouts that lost revenue. Dead inventory sitting on shelves for months because nobody had the framework to catch it early.

So I built one. A system that turns your sales data, inventory levels, and vendor history into clear, weekly purchasing recommendations — with the reasoning behind every line item.

FAQ

What does "fractional" mean?

I work as a part-time, embedded inventory planner for your team. You get expert-level planning without paying a full-time salary.

What's included in the free audit?

I'll analyze your sales data, current stock levels, and purchasing patterns. You'll get a report showing exactly where you're losing money — overbought SKUs, stockout risks, and cash trapped in dead stock. No obligation.

Why not just hire a Demand Planner?

A good one costs $80k+ plus benefits. I provide the same output — weekly buy lists, demand forecasts, inventory audits — for a fraction of that cost on a monthly retainer.

What if your recommendation is wrong?

You always approve orders. My role is to prevent blind spots — not replace your control. Every recommendation comes with quantified reasoning.

Do you replace my inventory software?

No. I build the decision layer on top of it. I pull data from your current tools, run the logic, and give you the answers.

How long do clients typically stay?

Most clients stay 6–18 months. Inventory planning is ongoing, not a one-time fix. But there's no lock-in — you retain everything built during setup.
